Formatting Guide

Answers should be wrapped with _underscores_.

Prompts should be wrapped with __double underscores__.

Italicize titles by using ~tildes~.

Pronunciation guides use (parentheses), unless the parentheses are escaped with \(backslashes\).

Pronunciation-guide targets (optional) wrap the word or words a guide covers in \P markers: Denis \PDiderot\P ("DID-er-OW"). The marked words render in a subtle teal in formatted views and Word export, and the document view can show the guide directly above them ("PGs Above"). The markers can span multiple words and never count toward question length. In the rich-text editor you can select the word(s) together with the following ("...") guide and click the PG button to add the markers; the style checker's pronunciation-guide suggestions add them automatically.

Superscripts follow the format \SMy Superscript Text\S.

Subscripts follow the format \sMy subscript text\s.

Powers (15 points) end with (*).

Superpowers (20 points, optional) end with (+), placed earlier in the question than the (*). A tossup may use both: text before (+) is worth 20, between (+) and (*) is worth 15.

Read Answer Carefully tag should be checked for answers with complex answer lines. When packetized, these answers will be flagged at the start of the packet.

Pasting plain text: the editor keeps formatting when you paste from Word or Google Docs. To paste without formatting, use Ctrl+Shift+V (Windows/Linux) or ⌘+Shift+V (Mac).
